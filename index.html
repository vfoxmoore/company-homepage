Our Credo

SGoMA is portal to inspiration that evokes creative thought and response. It is representative of the human condition and perceptions of our shared experience. We believe that art should be accessible and immersive for all. Come, unlock your creative self with us.

Gallery/Exhibitions

SGoMa is a melange of modern artistic styles. Stark impressionistic and subtly invoking works combine synergistically to bring the viewer along on a unique and fascinating journey. Each enocunter is a fresh new experience.

Classes

At SGoMa, our Master Artists enjoy creating a fun and informative experience for all skill levels. We try to keep it fresh by offering new and varied experiences on a regular basis. You'll enjoy playing with an array of media, styles and techniques. Don't forget to ask about student incentives and showings!
